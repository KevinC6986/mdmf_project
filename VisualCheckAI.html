Detection Signals

Our Mission

Generative AI tools like Midjourney, DALL·E, Sora, and Stable Diffusion can produce hyper-realistic images and videos that are increasingly difficult to distinguish from real content. VisualCheckAI aims to close that gap with a transparent, explainable detection pipeline.

The current version uses a placeholder detection model for demonstration purposes. The real model is under active development and will be integrated in a future release. We are committed to publishing accuracy benchmarks, model cards, and methodology documentation upon release.

Detection Signals

Our planned detection pipeline combines multiple signals to produce a robust, interpretable verdict:

  • Visual Pattern Recognition
  • Artifact & Glitch Detection
  • Metadata Verification
  • Frequency Fingerprinting
  • Temporal Consistency (Video)
  • Edge & Texture Analysis

Placeholder Model Notice

All detection scores shown are currently generated by a placeholder algorithm and do not reflect real AI-detection analysis. They exist solely to demonstrate the product interface. Do not use these results to make real-world decisions. A real model will replace this placeholder in a future release.
